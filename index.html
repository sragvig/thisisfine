The Journey

From an idea to a nationally-recognized project — here's how ThisIsFine came to life.

🏛️

HCI Workshops @ UT Austin

Learned human-centered design principles to shape the game's UX.

🏅

Civics Unplugged Mentorship

Received mentorship and civic tech guidance to ground the project in real community need.

🗺️

Planning w/ Fort Bend Emergency Management

Collaborated with emergency management professionals to ensure accuracy.

💻

First Draft!

Built the initial prototype with disaster categories and quiz mechanics.

📱

Cross-Device Testing & Mobile Version

Tested across devices and shipped a polished mobile-friendly build.

🎤

Final Presentation to Emergency Management Teams

Presented to teams across 7 states; adopted as a community resource.

🌐

Published on Official Website

Launched publicly for schools, families, and communities nationwide.

🎒

Packing 250 Preparedness Kits!

Assembled and distributed 250 physical emergency prep kits to students.

📣

Tabling Events & Workshops

Hit 37+ schools, tabling events, and teacher feedback sessions to keep improving.
